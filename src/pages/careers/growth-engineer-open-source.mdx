<AboutQuestdb />

## The Role

We are one the most popular time series databases according to the independent
reviewer DBEngines, but are reaching for the stars. Your role will consist of
orchestrating, monitoring and executing strategies that will impact the growth
of our open source communities, drive traffic to our website, and social
platforms (YouTube, Twitter, Reddit, etc.), and ultimately increase awareness.

We are looking for an individual who is passionate about Open Source, and driven
to spark continuous awareness of a deep-technology product. We recognise the
value of an open, creative, and collaborative individual who is excited about
interacting with developer communities on a daily basis.

This is an open-ended role with a substantial degree of autonomy. You will own
the end-to-end development of QuestDB’s growth strategy to increase adoption. In
addition to planning and participating in any other initiative to grow the
awareness and presence of QuestDB in open source and other tech communities.

## Responsibilities

- Orchestrating, monitoring, and executing growth strategies (paid & organic)
  for online and offline campaigns.
- Owning content pipeline, by planning, prioritising, creating, and distributing
  content to grab developers’ attention.
- Managing QuestDB’s online presence on our website, LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit,
  Youtube and other channels.
- Identifying growth metrics, and owning subsequent next steps: A/B test, build
  dashboards, track metrics, report insights, etc.
- Overseeing content strategy, based on new trends on Reddit and/or Github to
  assure organic growth.

## Requirements

- 6+ years of experience in growth hacking, technical marketing, software
  engineering, developer relations, community management, and/or technical
  product marketing.
- Proven hands-on experience owning growth strategies from start to finish for
  B2B SaaS companies.
- Experience managing, and distributing content on Social Media, websites,
  newsletters, etc.
- Entrepreneurial mindset
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Strong team player who is open to guiding colleagues on your areas of
  expertise
- Familiarity working with time series databases and proficiency in at least one
  common programming language are a plus
